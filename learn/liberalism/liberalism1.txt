liberal-liberalism-favorable to progress or reform, as in political or religious affairs. noting or pertaining to a political party advocating measures of progressive political reform.a person of liberal principles or views, especially in politics or a member of a liberal party in politics, especially of the Liberal party in Great Britain.the quality or state of being liberal, as in behavior or attitude.
a political or social philosophy advocating the freedom of the individual, parliamentary systems of government, nonviolent modification of political, social, or economic institutions to assure unrestricted development in all spheres of human endeavor, and governmental guarantees of individual rights and civil liberties.
