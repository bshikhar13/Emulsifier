genius-an exceptional natural capacity of intellect, especially as shown in creative and original work in science, art, music, etc.the genius of Mozart.Synonyms: intelligence, ingenuity, wit; brains.a person having an extraordinarily high intelligence rating on a psychological test, as an IQ above 140.Synonyms: mental giant, master, expert; whiz, brain, brainiac.
Antonyms: idiot, imbecile, half-wit, dope, moron; fool, simpleton, dunce, dullard, dolt; numskull, blockhead, nitwit, ninny.natural ability or capacity; strong inclination:
a special genius for leadership.
Synonyms: gift, talent, aptitude, faculty, endowment, predilection; penchant, knack, bent, flair, wizardry.
