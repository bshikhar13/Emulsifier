artist-a person who produces works in any of the arts that are primarily subject to aesthetic criteria.a person who practices one of the fine arts, especially a painter or sculptor.a person whose trade or profession requires a knowledge of design, drawing, painting, etc.:a commercial artist.a person who works in one of the performing arts, as an actor, musician, or singer; a public performer:a mime artist; an artist of the dance.a person whose work exhibits exceptional skill.a person who is expert at trickery or deceit:He's an artist with cards.Obsolete. an artisan.
